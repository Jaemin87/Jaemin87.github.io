The Hybrid Intelligent Experimental Robotics (HIER) Lab aims to study both theoretical and experimental research in the legged loco-manipulation of robotic systems for real-world deployment. We focus on
1) studying fundamentals to achive agile locomotion and dexterous manipulation
2) formulating planning and control frameworks for safety-critical autonomy
3) developing new hardware for more efficient and versatile mission completion
4) deploying learning technique to obtain adaptible solutions to challenging problem in unstructured environemtns.
